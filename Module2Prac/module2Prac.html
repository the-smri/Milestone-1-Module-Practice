Our Village

Our village is a peaceful and beautiful place surrounded by green fields and tall trees. Most of the people in our village are farmers, and they work hard to grow crops throughout the year. There is a small river near the village where children often play and people wash and bathe. Everyone knows one another, and villagers help each other in times of need. There is a primary school, a mosque, and a small market in our village. Life in the village is simple, calm, and close to nature, which makes it very special to us.

Pythagoras: The Father of Harmony and Number

The Early Life of a Seeker

Pythagoras was born around 570 BCE on the Greek island of Samos. The son of a gem-engraver, he grew up in a world of trade and craftsmanship, but his interests lay in the deeper mysteries of the cosmos. According to ancient historians, Pythagoras was an insatiable student who traveled throughout Egypt, Chaldea, and Babylon. During these journeys, he is believed to have studied with the leading mathematicians and priests of the time, absorbing the esoteric knowledge of the Near East and the advanced geometry of the Egyptians.
